Established in 1997, ALA Constructions is a Chennai-based real estate enterprise that has solidified its presence in the residential market with more than 80+ landmark properties across the Chennai, India. Since its inception, ALA has worked towards a vision of building aspirations and delivering value. Over 40,000 happy families have found their dream homes and investments at ALA Constructions. Having developed over 18 million sq. ft. of luxury residences across Chennai, Coimbatore and Kodaikanal, ALA has changed the landscape of South India with self- sufficient townships, premium apartments, independent villas and plots. ALA Constructions is among the best property developers in Chennai, with projects worth 8,000 crores including luxury apartments in Chennai and luxury villas in Chennai.
